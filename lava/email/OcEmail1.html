Welcome to Bayside Orange County!

Thank you for filling out a connect card at Bayside. We are thrilled you did! As you may know, Bayside has been around for 25 years with Orange County being its first campus down in the OC area. We are grateful to be on Vanguard’s campus, located in the center of some fantastic communities.
